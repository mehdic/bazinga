## 🚀 Quick Start (3 Steps)

### 1. Prepare (30 minutes)
- [ ] Read `MARKETING_STRATEGY.md` (overview)
- [ ] Create feedback form using `FEEDBACK_SYSTEM.md` questions
- [ ] Set up Calendly for user interviews
- [ ] Optional: Record 2-min demo video

### 2. Customize Content (1 hour)
- [ ] Open `SOCIAL_MEDIA_POSTS.md`
- [ ] Add your feedback form link
- [ ] Customize posts with your voice
- [ ] Add demo video/GIF links if available
- [ ] Prepare 3-5 posts for launch day

### 3. Launch (2-3 hours active time)
- [ ] Follow `LAUNCH_CHECKLIST.md` timeline
- [ ] Post on 3-5 platforms (Twitter, HN, Reddit)
- [ ] Monitor and respond to comments
- [ ] Collect initial feedback

**That's it! You're launched. 🎉**

## 📊 What Success Looks Like

### Week 1 Goals
- 10-20 people try BAZINGA
- 3-5 survey responses
- 1-2 GitHub issues/discussions
- 50+ social media engagements

### Week 6 Decision Point
**Continue IF:**
- ✅ 10+ active users returning
- ✅ 5+ "would miss it" responses
- ✅ Clear use case emerging
- ✅ Positive word-of-mouth

**Pivot or Pause IF:**
- ❌ <5 real users
- ❌ Lukewarm feedback
- ❌ No one returns after first try
- ❌ No clear problem being solved

See `MARKETING_STRATEGY.md` for full decision framework.

---

## 🎯 Key Principles

### 1. Feedback > Stars
10 detailed responses are more valuable than 1000 GitHub stars.

### 2. Quality > Quantity
5 passionate users beat 100 tire-kickers.

### 3. Learn Fast
6 weeks is enough to know if there's something here.

### 4. Be Honest
If nobody wants this, that's valuable data.

### 5. Stay Humble
You're looking for feedback, not selling a product.

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Do:
✅ Respond to every comment within 2 hours (first 48h)
✅ Thank people for trying it
✅ Be vulnerable about uncertainties
✅ Share learnings publicly
✅ Fix bugs quickly
✅ Ask clarifying questions

### Don't:
❌ Oversell ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
❌ Ignore negative feedback
❌ Get defensive
❌ Spam multiple platforms at once
❌ Post and disappear
❌ Argue with critics

## ❓ FAQ

### "When should I launch?"
**Monday morning (9-11am PT)** is ideal for US audience. Wednesday works too.

### "How much time will this take?"
- Launch day: 3-4 hours active monitoring
- Week 1: 1-2 hours/day responding
- Ongoing: 3-5 hours/week

### "What if no one responds?"
Check `LAUNCH_CHECKLIST.md` "If Things Go Quiet" section. Often means unclear value prop or wrong audience.

### "What if I get harsh criticism?"
That's valuable! Thank them, ask clarifying questions, and learn. See response templates in `SOCIAL_MEDIA_POSTS.md`.

### "Should I pay for ads?"
Not yet. Organic feedback is more valuable early on. Paid ads come after product-market fit.
